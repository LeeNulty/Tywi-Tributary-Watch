Interpretation

The paired results show a clear downstream increase in phosphate, nitrate and ammonia at this sampling time. That pattern is consistent with a local nutrient input between the two sites.

This single comparison cannot by itself establish the source, frequency or long-term scale of the input. Repeat paired sampling under different flows and conditions would test whether the pattern persists.

Suggested next steps

Repeat the same upstream/downstream comparison during lower summer flow and after rainfall, then add macroinvertebrate sampling at both sites to assess whether chemical differences are accompanied by a biological response.
